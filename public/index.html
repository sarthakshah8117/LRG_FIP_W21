London Referees Group

Welcome to

The London Referees Group(LRG) is an organization of more than 225 hockey referees, serving London and surrounding area. LRG is a structure that is comprised of seven member partners: Hockey Canada, Ontario Hockey Federation, Alliance, Ontario Minor Hockey Association, Ontario Women's Hockey Association, Ontario Hockey Association, and Sledge Hockey.

For ages, the LRG provides an opportunity to Junior members, Co-ordinates programs, administrative resources, services, and events for hockey participants and the members. If you are looking for an organization to referee your Hockey league, our team would like the opportunity to assist.
