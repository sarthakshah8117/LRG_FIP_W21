Programs

Welcome to London Referees Group programs. Here you will find alternatives on how you can get involved in hockey. We want all Canadians involved in the game, so we are creating more possibilities for more people to play hockey in many more ways.

From big cities to small towns, adult to youth leagues and driveway pick-up, volunteer coaches to team managers and officials - if you play this sport, in this country, you are our most important focus.

Coaches are essential to hockey at every level. A caring, enthusiastic, well-trained coach can be positive influence on the experience of players, parents and other coaches.

London Referees Group works with its members partners and local hockey associations to provide effective education, certification and registration for the juniors.
